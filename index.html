Welcome to Swami Vivekanand Institute of Polytechnic, where excellence in education is our hallmark. Technical Education is essential for the economic growth of any society, as it helps build human capital equipped with the requisite knowledge, skill and values necessary for progress. Our students acquire the capability and strength to cope with challenges that lie ahead in their pursuit of a successful future.

India is fast emerging as the talent hub for the global knowledge economy. J.S.P.M. Group of Institute's Latur is proud of partnering in this national endeavor by delivering quality education towards evolving holistic competence to young students seeking career opportunities in the globalized corporate world.

If you are looking for an exciting and rewarding career in the increasingly hi-tech worlds of the 21st century, a Diploma education is the right choice and our institution is the right place to be. Our Polytechnic, while proud of its top-quality teaching and academic achievements, continues to emphasis its tradition of service to society. We are sure that you, as an aspiring student or faculty, will find our institution meets with your satisfaction, as our sole aim is to provide the finest possible environment both for learning and teaching.
