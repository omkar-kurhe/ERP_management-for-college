About Us

Innovative Way To Learn

Swami Vivekanand Institute of Polytechnic is situated in Latur in Maharashtra state of India. Established in 2011, SVI is a Private college. The college is affiliated with Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur and accredited by AICTE. Swami Vivekanand Institute of Polytechnic offers 5 courses across 1 streams namely Engineering..Popular degrees offered at Swami Vivekanand Institute of Polytechnic include Diploma..Besides a robust teaching pedagogy, Swami Vivekanand Institute of Polytechnic is also a leader in research and innovation.Focus is given to activities beyond academics at Swami Vivekanand Institute of Polytechnic, which is evident from its infrastructure, extracurricular activities and national & international collaborations. The placement at Swami Vivekanand Institute of Polytechnic is varied, with recruitment options both in corporates and public sector as well as entrepreneurship.
